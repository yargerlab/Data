# FT-IR of Gases

A Thermo Scientific Nicolet iS20 FTIR Spectrometer with standard gas cell setup is commonly used by the ASU physical chemistry labs to acquire gas phase FT-IR data (spectra).  Here we provide some publicly available data collected on this instrument.  Much of the data is associated with doing FT-IR on the exit gases from the 'cloud' (remote) acoustic interferometry setup developed by Prof. Yarger, Dr. Samrat Amin and Dr. Josh Jeffs at ASU.
